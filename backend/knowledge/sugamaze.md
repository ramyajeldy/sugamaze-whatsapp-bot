# Sugamaze – The Cake Shop

## About
Sugamaze (Sugamaze Inc) is a Toronto / Durham Region–based bakery specializing in
100% eggless custom cakes and desserts. Handcrafted cakes for every occasion,
serving the Greater Toronto Area and Durham Region, Ontario. The bakery was
founded by Srivani, the creative baker behind Sugamaze, known for custom,
celebration, and eggless cakes.

## Cake care & safety guidelines
- Each cake is individually handcrafted, so minor variations from photos are normal,
  and colors may look different in person than on screens.
- Remove all internal dowel/support rods from tiered cakes before cutting and serving.
- Always remove decorative elements before serving; some may contain non-edible
  materials that are a choking risk, especially for children.
- Strawberry cakes (with fresh strawberries inside or as decoration) should be eaten
  the same day for best quality and safety.
- Dark colors (black, navy, deep red) may taste slightly bitter and can temporarily
  stain hands or lips; lighter alternatives are available, or dark frosting can be
  removed before eating.
- Always supervise young children when serving decorated cakes.
